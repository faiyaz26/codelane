## What the AGPL-3.0 Requires (Why You'd Want Commercial)

Under AGPL-3.0, you **must**:
- Keep all modifications open source under AGPL-3.0

- Allow users to download and modify your version

## Legal Terms Summary

Commercial licenses typically include:

- **Support:** Technical support as specified in tier
- **Warranty:** Limited warranty on the software (enterprise tiers)
- **Liability:** Standard commercial liability limitations
